  • A - Shows all to do.
  • C - Shows completed to do.
  • U - Shows uncompleted to do.
  • K - Show/Hide the shortcuts.
  • 1-9 - Complete or delete a specific todo (works in order).
  • ENTER - Focus the input to add the todo.
  • ENTER - Add todo to the list (once it's written).
  • ESC - Deselects the text field.
